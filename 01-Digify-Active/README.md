# 🌟 Digify Active – B2B Marketing Agency Project

## 🌟 Executive Summary
A team-based academic project focused on building and testing a B2B marketing agency from scratch. The objective was to simulate a realistic go-to-market (GTM) process, combining strategic positioning, UX/UI, SEO, paid acquisition, and performance analysis.

### **The Problem**
Many Small and Medium-Sized (SME) fitness businesses lack structured digital marketing capabilities and rely on fragmented acquisition channels. This creates an opportunity to design a marketing service tailored to their operational constraints.

### **The Solution**
Designed a marketing agency that provides:
- **Strategic Positioning:** A clear and compelling value proposition for SME fitness owners.
- **UX/UI & SEO:** A professional website optimized for search engine visibility and conversion.
- **Paid Acquisition:** Multi-channel campaigns (Google Ads, Meta) with tracking and optimization.
- **Performance Analysis:** KPI dashboards for real-time visibility into cost-per-lead and ROI.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills
- **Skills:** Marketing Strategy, Brand Identity, SEO, SEM, Performance Analytics, Team Management.
- **Tools:** Google Ads, HubSpot, Google Search Console, SEMRush, Canva (UI), Excel (KPI tracking).

---

## 📊 Methodology
1. **Market Research:** Identified key pain points for fitness business owners.
2. **Branding & UX:** Developed a trust-focused brand identity and a high-converting website layout.
3. **GTM Strategy:** Defined target segments and messaging for LinkedIn and cold outreach.
4. **Campaign Testing:** Ran pilot campaigns to test messaging resonance and acquisition costs.
5. **Insights & Iteration:** Analyzed data to optimize lead quality and reduce CPL.

---

## 💡 Results & Recommendations
- **Simulated GTM:** Successfully moved from concept to functional agency model.
- **KPI Focus:** Shifted from broad traffic goals to high-intent lead generation.
- **Recommendation:** Scaling through automated HubSpot sequences and localized SEO strategies.
